Flights

Direct flights to Egypt are available year-round. Main destinations are Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh. Flight time is about 4-5 hours.

Insurance

Medical insurance is mandatory for travel to Egypt. We recommend choosing a policy with coverage of at least $35,000 and including active recreation (diving, snorkeling).
